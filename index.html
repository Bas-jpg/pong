pong is a game where there are 2 players player 1 has control over theyr paddle with W (up) and S (down), player 2 has control over theyr paddle with Arrow Key up (up) and Arrow Key down (down).

when the ball goes past a paddle behind a player the other side's player will score a point.

the ball will accelerate when it touches a paddle and this velocity is capped at 15 or -15.
